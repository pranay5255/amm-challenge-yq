# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Provide helpful feedback on pull requests and issues
- Share benchmark results and findings openly
- Credit others' work and ideas appropriately
- Focus on the technical merits of contributions

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information
- Deliberately submitting misleading benchmark results
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this repository.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
